The 'artery' of carbon-neutral: energy storage

July 20, 2020 · Report · Huatai Securities
*This article was selected by the management out of 6 candidate articles to be presented in the director's weekly business streaming to clients which achieved a remarkable 11,000 views.
Abstract: The main path to ultimate carbon neutrality is through developing renewable energy such as photovoltaic and wind power to replace fossile fuel. However, renewable energy such as photovoltaic and wind power are typical intermittent energy that are unpractical to utilize without replying on energy storage. Besides, due to the highly volatile power generation capacity of renewable energy, significant damage will be caused to grid in the long run. As a result, the allocation of energy storage device can also play an important role of buffering for grid.
Put it simply, the great blueprint of carbon neutrailty cannot be achieved without the common advance of renewable energy and energy storage technology.
I reintroduce the topic of energy storage to clients in this literature, analyse its growth potential with the continuous development of renewable energy. More importantly, the biggest contribution of this literature is the analysis of the 'Go' between second-line battery firms and first-line giants.
